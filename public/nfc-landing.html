The Chip

Cryptographic Signing

Every tap sends a unique CMAC signature generated from the chip's locked AES-128 key. The server rejects anything not freshly signed.

Monotonic Counter

The chip's counter increments every read and can never decrease. Replayed links are rejected instantly.

Hardware-Locked Key

The AES key lives in the chip's secure memory — it cannot be read out even with professional equipment. The card is the key.
